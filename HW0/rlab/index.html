New Unity Project

Rolling-A-Ball Game!

Introduction to the game

There are two game mode. Both of them can be played with two players in one keyboard.

Survival Mode

Player 1 acts as the ball, collecting cubes and avoiding fatal egg-like creatures. Player 2 controls the base, tilts it to let creatures moves. P2 can either help P1 achieve the goal or inverse. Take anyone you like.

Keymapping:

  • [wasd]: Tilting the base.
  • [up, down, left, right]: move the ball.
  • [space]: Jump.
  • [R]: Short cut to restart the game.

Loot Mode

2 Players loot the ground, and compete their scores!

Scoring Rules:

  • Loot a cube: +1
  • touch a wall or red stone: -1
  • Jump attack!: receiver -1
  • Scores won't be less than 0

Keymapping:

  • [wasd]: Player 1 moving.
  • [space]: Player 1 jump.
  • [up, down, left, right]: Player 2 moving.
  • [R-ctrl]: Player 2 jump.
  • [R]: Short cut to restart the game.

Other features

  • Camera follows the player & minimap.
  • Double jump, more exciting action!
